Privacy Policy

Fret Master — Electrifying Software — Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Fret Master ("we", "our", or "the app") is a guitar learning tool developed by Electrifying Software. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Microphone Access

Fret Master requests access to your device microphone solely to provide the chromatic tuner feature. Audio is processed entirely on-device in real time. No audio data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

3. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app stores only your in-app preferences (note display style, difficulty settings, metronome BPM) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

4. No Network Access

Fret Master does not require or use an internet connection. No data is sent to or received from external servers, third-party services, or analytics platforms.

5. No Third-Party SDKs

The app does not include any advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Fret Master does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will publish the revised version at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
